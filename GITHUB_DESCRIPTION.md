# Suggested Short Pitch

A concept architecture for a human-supervised AI customer-service cockpit. The system lets admins observe both the live AI-customer conversation and the AI’s structured decision space, then intervene when business judgment is needed.

# Suggested Public Positioning

This repository documents an early-stage architecture concept, not a production-ready product. It is intended to show practical thinking around AI agents, customer-service automation, human oversight, AI governance, and business conversion workflows.
